About the Company

BaltTransService LLC (BTS) was founded in 1999 in Saint Petersburg.

BaltTransService is one of the largest private operators specializing in the rail transport of liquid petroleum products in Russia and the CIS.

We operate our own train formations on the North, Moscow, October, Kuybyshev, North-Caucasus, South-East and Kaliningrad railway lines.

Offices in Moscow, Ryazan, Yaroslavl, Nizhnekamsk, Anzhero-Sudzhensk, Velikiye Luki and Dno; wagon repair depot in Ivanovo; locomotive repair subsidiary “RemTransService” in Rybinsk; BTS Locomotive Solutions.

Recipient of multiple industry awards for quality, safety and responsible business practices.

Our History

1999

BTS begins operations as a rail operator of liquid petroleum products across Russia and the Baltics.

2001

Joins the Association of Carriers and Operators; wins industry awards.

2004

Launches own train formations from Novoyaroslavskaya station on the Northern Railway.

2005

Expands routes on the Northern, October, Moscow and North-Caucasus lines.

2008

Acquires wagon repair depot in Ivanovo; opens 8 regional offices.

2009

Becomes part of Globaltrans Group, a regional freight leader.

2010

Creates RemTransService locomotive repair subsidiary in Rybinsk.

2012

Increases mainline locomotive fleet to 60 units.

2017–2022

Delivers new locomotives, launches wheelset workshops, expands tank car fleet by over 6,000 units.
